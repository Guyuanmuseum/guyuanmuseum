Family-authorized international cultural archive

家族正式授權之國際文化檔案項目

English

This online museum and archival project is established with the authorization and support of Gu Yuan's family.

The project is developed in collaboration with Gu Ancun (古安村), daughter of the renowned Chinese printmaking master Gu Yuan (古元), and supports the cultural and artistic continuation of the Gu Yuan artistic legacy in Toronto, Canada.

The initiative also supports Gu Yuan's granddaughter Duan Wei (段薇) and her four children in preserving, promoting, researching, and developing the international cultural presence of Gu Yuan's art for future generations.

This platform aims to serve as:

  • A digital museum and research archive
  • An educational and cultural platform
  • A bridge between Chinese printmaking history and international audiences
  • A future foundation for exhibitions, publications, educational programs, and cultural exchange in Canada

繁體中文

本線上美術館與數位檔案館項目，經古元家族正式授權與支持建立。

本項目由中國著名版畫藝術大師「古元」之女 古安村女士 協力支持，並致力於在加拿大多倫多推動古元藝術文化的國際傳承與發展。

同時，本項目亦支持古元外孫女 段薇女士 與其四位子女，共同參與古元藝術的保存、研究、推廣與未來國際文化發展工作。

本平台未來將作為：

  • 古元藝術數位美術館與研究檔案庫
  • 國際藝術教育與文化交流平台
  • 中國新興版畫歷史的重要海外展示窗口
  • 未來展覽、出版、學術研究與加拿大文化推廣的重要基礎

Artist Timeline 藝術家年表

A clean chronological pathway helps visitors understand Gu Yuan's movement from Guangdong to Yan'an, from revolutionary printmaking to national art education. 清晰的年代脈絡，協助觀眾理解古元從廣東走向延安，並由革命木刻實踐延伸至國家美術教育的重要歷程。

1919 Born in Guangdong, China
生於中國廣東
1938 Went to Yan'an and entered the revolutionary art environment
赴延安，進入革命文藝環境
1940 Graduated from Lu Xun Academy of Literature and Art
畢業於魯迅藝術文學院
1940s Created defining Yan'an woodcuts rooted in local life
創作以地方生活為根基的延安木刻代表作品
1959 Joined Central Academy of Fine Arts as an educator
任教於中央美術學院，投身美術教育
1996 Passed away, leaving a major printmaking legacy
辭世，留下重要的版畫藝術遺產

Museum Strategy 博物館策展方向

Built for collectors, scholars, students, and the public. 為收藏家、學者、學生與公眾而建立

The website should feel like a serious archive, not a commercial shop. Use museum-level metadata, large quiet images, bilingual captions, and a strong research section.

網站整體應呈現國際級線上博物館氣質，而非商業販售平台。建議使用大尺寸留白影像、雙語說明、完整作品資料與研究型內容架構。

Future functions can include advanced search, interactive map, exhibition mode, educational downloads, provenance notes, and collection-rights management.

未來可擴展功能包括：作品資料庫檢索、互動展覽地圖、教育下載資源、收藏來源記錄，以及版權與授權管理系統。
