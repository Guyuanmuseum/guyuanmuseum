# Gu Yuan Digital Museum Website Editorial Standards

## Website Editorial Rule #001

No ending `。` or `.` in titles, headings, navigation items, captions, labels, slogans, taglines, gallery names, or footer fragments.

Use punctuation only for complete sentences, paragraph text, quotations, article body text, news content, and formal descriptive text.

This rule applies to both English and Traditional Chinese content. It must be applied to future content additions, translations, page updates, metadata, captions, banners, navigation menus, gallery titles, and footer elements.

Examples:

- Correct: `古元數字美術館`
- Correct: `中國現代記憶的木刻史詩`
- Correct: `The Carved Memory of Modern China`
- Correct: `Explore Collection`
- Incorrect: `古元數字美術館。`
- Incorrect: `中國現代記憶的木刻史詩。`
- Incorrect: `The Carved Memory of Modern China.`
- Incorrect: `Explore Collection.`

## Website Typography Rule #002

Headings and titles must use balanced line breaks across desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.

Never leave a single Chinese character on a line by itself. Avoid isolated final English words whenever the layout can reasonably prevent it.

Examples:

- Correct: `中國現代記憶的木刻史詩`
- Correct: `中國現代記憶的 / 木刻史詩`
- Incorrect: `中國現代記憶的木刻史 / 詩`
- Correct: `The Carved Memory / of Modern China`
- Correct: `The Carved Memory of / Modern China`
- Incorrect: `The Carved Memory of Modern / China`

## Website Footer Standard #003

Use the institutional museum footer structure as the permanent footer standard:

- `Gu Yuan Digital Museum`
- `A Digital Archive of Modern Chinese Visual Culture`
- `© 2026 Gu Yuan Digital Museum All Rights Reserved`
- `A digital preservation initiative developed by OWL Art Foundation`
- `Website design and platform copyright © 2026 OWL Art Foundation`

Keep the footer centered, with the museum name visually prominent and OWL Art Foundation credit lines slightly smaller than the museum identity.

The OWL logo may appear as a small sharp mark only. Do not add duplicate `OWL Art Foundation` text underneath the logo.

Use the approved final dark-background OWL logo artwork for the footer and future OWL Art Foundation branding areas. The logo must stay centered, sharp, fully visible, and must never crop the owl, leaves, WL letters, or circular line. Display it as a small institutional credit mark so it does not compete with the Gu Yuan Digital Museum identity. Do not add duplicate `OWL Art Foundation` wording underneath the logo.
